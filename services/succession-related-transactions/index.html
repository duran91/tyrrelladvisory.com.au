Other Succession Related Transactions

An increasing number of private businesses are facing succession challenges, particularly where there is a desire from the founder’s next generation to acquire part or all of the business. Tyrrell Advisory works with all family members to develop a plan enabling the staged buy-out of the founder’s equity.

Similarly, we can advise parties in instances where management teams may be seeking to implement a Management Buy-Out (MBO) or Management Buy-In (MBI) of a business.

In each of the above transactions, Tyrrell Advisory can provide the following services:

  • Deal structuring and transaction management
  • Valuing the business
  • Working together with the various stakeholders
  • Co-ordinate with other advisors and financiers
  • Document the transaction
  • Close the transaction
