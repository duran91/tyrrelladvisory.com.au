Acquisitions

A successful acquisition requires the development of growth strategies, rigorous planning and project management. Tyrrell Advisory can provide a range of associated services in relation to acquisitions, including:

  • Research potential acquisition targets
  • Approach targets on a no-names basis to explore target’s willingness to engage
  • Manage introductions and release of information
  • Assessment of value and deal structuring
  • Negotiation strategy
  • Co-ordinating with our client’s other advisors, including solicitors, due diligence providers and financiers
  • Closing the transaction

Relevant Acquisitions

Advised CHAMP Ventures

David was heavily involved in advising the private equity firm, CHAMP Ventures, on the establishment of a national fire services organisation (Spectrum Fire) via the acquisition of three State-based privately-owned businesses plus subsequent bolt-on acquisitions.

Various Acquisition Engagements

In recent years we have advised a number of companies looking to grow and/or diversify their activities by acquiring other businesses.

Examples of sectors in which we have assisted clients include air-conditioning (where we represented a global organisation in its search activities), steel fabrication, giftware and freight forwarding.

In many instances we have undertaken an initial detailed research exercise to identify acquisition targets, always taking into consideration the preferred parameters of our clients.

On other occasions we have simply worked with clients to approach specific targets identified by our clients.
