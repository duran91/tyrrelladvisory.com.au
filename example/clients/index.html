Our Clients

Our clients encompass a broad spectrum of the business community. In particular we work closely with:

  • Private business owners across a wide range of industries including industrial markets, retail, distribution, education and professional services
  • Family Offices
  • Private investors and investment groups
  • Private equity firms
  • Mid-market corporates and smaller listed companies

In addition, we have an extensive network among legal firms, the major accounting and tax firms, banking and financial institutions, and other advisory firms.
