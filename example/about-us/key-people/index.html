David Tyrrell

Managing Director

David is an M&A specialist with a strong track record from over 25 years’ experience advising clients (mainly private business owners) and managing transactions across a broad range of industries and jurisdictions.

0421 922 057

david@tyrrelladvisory.com.au

Background

Prior to founding Tyrrell Advisory, David co-founded Lawson Advisory Partners, and was previously Director of Corporate Advisory at Deloitte Private, Director at McGrathNicol (where inter-alia he led the sales team in relation to the divestment of the various businesses comprising Nylex Limited), Director at National Australia Bank (where he headed up the newly established Succession Services team in Melbourne – an M&A unit advising corporate and business banking customers) and Director at KPMG Corporate Finance (where he headed up the Middle Market M&A Group in Melbourne).

He has extensive experience in lead advisory transactions, including business divestitures and acquisitions, corporate recovery, private equity and valuations. He has a unique and varied background gained in senior roles with financial institutions and professional services firms both in Australia and overseas. These experiences provide David with the unique ability to view transactions from the perspective of our clients.

David possesses a range of analytical, communication, negotiation, presentation and management skills and has a proven track record in advising on, and completing, a range of acquisition and divestment transactions across a wide range of industry sectors in Australia, United Kingdom, United States, New Zealand, Poland, Hungary and Hong Kong.

David holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree from the University of Melbourne and is a qualified Chartered Accountant and a Fellow of the Financial Services Institute of Australasia. David is married with two daughters and enjoys travelling. He also enjoys golf and keeping fit (including having hiked the Kokoda Track) and is a proud Richmond supporter.
