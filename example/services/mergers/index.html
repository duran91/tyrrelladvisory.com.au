Mergers, Joint Ventures and Strategic Alliances

Generally, many of the services required for mergers, joint ventures and strategic alliances will replicate those required for acquisitions. In such transactions Tyrrell Advisory will assist the business owners through the entire process providing high level strategy and planning advice, deal structuring, project management and transaction execution services and in conjunction with our clients liaise and negotiate with counterparties.
